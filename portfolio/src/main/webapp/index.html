Introduction

My name is Neal Davar and I am a junior computer science student at The University of Texas at Austin. I have demonstrated experience in cross platform mobile app development (Flutter and React Native) and full stack web development (with what is traditionally known as the Microsoft stack => ASP.NET, Razor HTML, and the MVC design pattern).

Journey Into the World of Computer Science

My journey into the world of software development was somewhat atypical. I was formerly a pre-medical student with a burning desire to help people. However, more recently, I have realized I can obtain the satisfaction of helping people at a potentially larger scale through the world of computer science. This realization and reflection upon my characteristics of being a lifelong learner and someone who enjoys tinkering with new technologies led to me finding this field interesting. I hope working as a software developer in the industry will allow me to put these interests to use on a larger scale and potentially solve global issues.
