About Us

Our Mission

At Waves, we recognize the importance of mental health. In today's day and age, whenever we experience a mental health crisis, many of us turn to Google to look for answers. However, sometimes it can be difficult to find resources that will work for us for many reasons including finding too many resources and not knowing which one to choose, misinformation, and more. That's where we come in! We hope that our chatbot will be able to help you find the right resource for you.

Our Features

Currently our chat bot has the following features. If you have requests for other features or genera feedback, please feel free to fill out this form

  • Ability to recommend activities based on current mood
  • Ability to guide user to scheduling appointments with medical providers (provided through Zocdoc API)
